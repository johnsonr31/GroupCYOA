Space Academy Graduates

Welcome to the space academy. Here is our our story long, long time ago in a beautiful, beautiful galaxy... After leaving the cosy planet Byss, a group of goblins fly toward a distant speck. The speck gradually resolves into a grubby, space hut. Civil war strikes the galaxy, which is ruled by Boris Raymond, a cursed wizard capable of theft and even assassination. Terrified, a silver ghost known as Brad Willis flees the Empire, with his protector, Andy Pigeon. They head for London on the planet Dagobah. When they finally arrive, a fight breaks out. Pigeon uses his beautiful pistol to defend Brad. Pigeon and Ghost Brad decide it's time to leave Dagobah and steal a boat to shoot their way out. They encounter a tribe of trolls. Pigeon is attacked and the ghost is captured by the trolls and taken back to London. Pigeon must fight to save Ghost Brad but when he accidentally unearths an idyllic map, the entire future of the beautiful, cosy galaxy is at stake.
