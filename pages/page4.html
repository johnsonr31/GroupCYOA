Good Ending: Repairing the Ship


As the issues with the ship continue to get worse, you notice something that you recognize a bit in the issues. After asking some of the other team members, you realize that all of you know a few details which might help, though the details aren't as useful without being used together.

Everyone is relieved to see that they will survive the trip, and the ship continues heading to its destination. Hopefully there won't be too many more issues that arise after this…
