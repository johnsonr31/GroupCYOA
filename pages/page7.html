Bad Ending: Aggressive Teamwork


The rest of the team continues to butt heads with each other. In frustration, you take charge and aggressively convince them to start working together. The others are clearly not fond of the way you are harshly forcing them to comply, but they go along with it.

This seems to work well at first, as the rest of the team begins working together nicely. However, they begin to act rather aggressive and hostile towards you, even if they are working together much better now. The ship then makes a stop on a small planet, which isn't the one your ship was meant to head to. Before you can question the rest of the team, you find yourself being thrown out of the ship, which then flies away!

While you succeeded in convincing the team to work together, your methods weren't great, and you accidentally convinced the team to work together against you…
